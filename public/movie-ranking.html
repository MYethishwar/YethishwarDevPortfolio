The Best movies according to Yethishwar

My Top 5 movies of all time

Jersey

A poignant sports drama about a middle-aged cricketer's journey of redemption and determination to make a comeback, beautifully portrayed by Nani.

I

"I" is an action-packed Telugu film that follows the story of a charismatic bodybuilder who seeks revenge against those who have wronged him. Filled with thrilling fight sequences, romantic moments, and emotional twists

Maharshi

An inspiring story of a successful entrepreneur's introspective journey back to his roots, striving to uplift rural communities and rediscover his purpose, featuring Mahesh Babu in the lead role.

Eega

A visually stunning fantasy film directed by S.S. Rajamouli, depicting the reincarnation of a man as a housefly seeking vengeance against his murderer, with innovative storytelling and captivating visual effects.

Govindhudu Andarivadele

A family drama showcasing the bond between a man and his estranged family, directed by Krishna Vamsi and starring Ram Charan and Kajal Aggarwal in lead roles, exploring themes of love, tradition, and reconciliation.
